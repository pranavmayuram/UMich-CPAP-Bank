How can I qualify?


You may qualify for free equipment and/or supplies if:

  • You don’t have insurance coverage
  • Your insurance doesn’t cover durable medical equipment (DME)
  • You don’t have the funds to pay for the equipment or the copay

You or your health care provider can contact us directly. We will need a prescription for a machine and a copy of your sleep study.


Equipment Details


We have CPAP, BiPAP, auto-CPAP, auto-BiPAP, ASV (adaptive servo-ventilation) and AVAPS (average volume assured pressure support) machines at the Bank. And we have given more than 90 machines and various supplies to people who qualify for them.

University of Michigan MedEQUIP cleans and checks the equipment and makes sure it’s functioning properly. Then, it’s perfectly safe and sanitary for another person (you) to use the recycled machines.

To request a machine, fill out and submit the form below. To learn more, visit our Contact Us page. Thanks!
