Why?


CPAP machines can be extremely expensive, and many people who cannot afford them are without help for sleepless nights. CPAP machines must also be changed out for many regular sleep apnea patients. We saw an opportunity here for conservation, where owners of out-of-use CPAP machines could donate to us, and we could serve as a middle man to transfer these machines to people in need.


What does {{sitename}} accept?


If you are someone with a CPAP machine that is functioning, but no longer necessary for you, please consider donating. If you are a home care company we are also open to donations from you!

We take gently used equipment and supplies that are still in their original plastic packaging. Please note that we cannot take equipment from smoking households because we can’t get the smell out of the equipment.

University of Michigan MedEQUIP cleans and checks the equipment and makes sure it’s functioning properly. Then, it’s perfectly safe and sanitary for another person to use the recycled machines.
